Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Advanced Finance Tracker. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data. Your privacy is critically important to us.

2. Data Storage & Processing

All information you enter—transaction titles, amounts, categories, and dates—is stored solely in your browser's local storage. We do not operate any external databases, and we never transmit any of your financial data over the network to any server.

3. Use of Cookies

This website uses essential cookies to remember your preferences (theme settings, language selection, and cookie consent choice). Non-essential cookies (e.g., analytics) are only set if you explicitly accept them via our cookie banner.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services (analytics, ads, or social media). No data is shared externally.

5. Your Rights

Since all data remains in your local browser, you can view, delete, or export all your data at any time. Clearing your browser's site data will permanently remove all stored records.

6. Contact

If you have questions, contact your module instructor or TA.
